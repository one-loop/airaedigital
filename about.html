About Us

We're a leading digital marketing agency based in Colombo, Sri Lanka that specializes in providing marketing, social media management and web development services for businesses and individuals worldwide

We created Airae in 2022 to bridge the gap between businesses and the internet.

Amplify marginalized voices
Share stories with a heart
Build communities
Give back to the world
We're not in the business of selling at any cost or running after unrealistic, aggressive monthly numbers. We center people in everything we do and we love working with companies that share our ethos.

It takes a village to do most things and we want to ensure that we're always giving back to that village.
